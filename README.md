# Bitcoin Village Watch

## Bringing Bitcoin’s transparency to villages

Bitcoin taught us transparent money. We're applying the same to village resources — water quality and public funds.

---

## 🔍 The Problem

In thousands of villages:
- People pay taxes — but water is dirty
- Government funds are allocated — but never arrive
- No records, no accountability, no trust

---

## 💡 The Solution

We're building an **open-source AI system** that monitors:

- ✅ Water quality in real-time
- ✅ Public fund usage — where money goes
- ✅ Anomaly detection — corruption flagged instantly

Every finding is logged transparently — **just like Bitcoin's ledger**.

---

## 🌱 Why Bitcoin?

Bitcoin taught the world what **real transparency** looks like:
- 🔹 Immutable records
- 🔹 No central control
- 🔹 Trust through code, not promises

We're applying the **same philosophy** to village resources.
